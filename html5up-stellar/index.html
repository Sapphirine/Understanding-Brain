Brain Segmentation

nnUNet is trained and tested for the task. nnU-Net is a self-adapting framework for biomedical image segmentation that automates the configuration of neural networks to suit specific dataset requirements. By intelligently adjusting its architecture, data preprocessing, and training procedures based on the characteristics of the data being analyzed, nnU-Net eliminates the need for manual tuning and expedites the development of highly efficient segmentation models. This versatility allows it to achieve state-of-the-art performance across a wide range of segmentation tasks without requiring specialized adaptations for different medical imaging challenges.
